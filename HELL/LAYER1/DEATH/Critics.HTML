YOU CAN'T RUN FROM DEATH

You are done

You hate everything about this. No matter how HARD you hide it you just see it seep through the small cracks of light. You see your face. You feel like you HAVE to smother it. To hide that little light. "YOU DON'T DESERVE THIS." you suffocate the reflection. And by proxy, You.

Your reflection wakes up and says:

Run you can try

But you will always die
